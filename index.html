Lotus Flower

The lotus flower 🌸 is a beautiful aquatic plant that grows in ponds and lakes. It rises above muddy water and blooms with soft pink or white petals. The flower is known as a symbol of purity, beauty, and spiritual growth. Its large green leaves float on the water surface, creating a calm and natural scene. The lotus opens during the day and closes at night, making it a unique and fascinating flower.
