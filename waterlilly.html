WaterLilly

A water lily is a beautiful aquatic flower that grows on the surface of ponds and lakes. It has large round leaves that float on water. The flower blooms in colors like white, pink, yellow, and purple. Water lilies are known for their calm beauty and attractive appearance. 🌸
