Dalhia

A dahlia is a beautiful flower known for its round shape and many layered petals. It comes in bright colors like pink, red, yellow, purple, and white. Dahlias grow in gardens and are popular for their attractive and colorful appearance. They are often used for decoration and flower arrangements. This flower symbolizes beauty, elegance, and creativity. 🌸
